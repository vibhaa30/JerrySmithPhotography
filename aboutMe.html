About Jerry Smith

An avid photographer trying to make social change a reality!

Biography

Jerry Smith is an artist that takes social justice issues into account and spreads awareness through his collections. His parents were archaeologists, they traveled the world together. During these trips they would stop at various countries and he witnessed the struggles of different social classes, ethnicities, and cultures. Since he never got the chance to have a traditional school education, he spent most of his time discovering what he really enjoyed. While doing so, his passion for photography and helping those in need began to grow. In his time at college he wanted to show his perspective on current social issues so he studied journalism, pursuing photography on the side. As time went on, he fell more in love with expressing others and himself through photography. Now he is an full-time photographer sharing his experiences with you.

Along with this great passion, Jerry wants to help empower those with differing abilities through his work and website. In order to do so, the website offers ways to change the accessibility settings. Learn more about this feature here!

This website allows people like you to view all his different collections and educate yourself more on these topics. But you don't have to stop there! Book an appointment to discuss with Jerry or, if you would like, be featured in his future photographs!
